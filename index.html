I am a postdoc at QuTech, TU Delft. Previously, I was a postdoc at EPFL IC. I obtained my PhD from the Technical University of Darmstadt in October 2021. Before that, I did my master’s and bachelor’s at the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata. I am broadly interested in analyzing (quantum and classical) network performance using tools from probability theory.
